Recipe

Try this simple sautéed mushroom toast for a cozy weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • Fresh mushrooms
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Toast and herbs

Steps

  1. Warm a pan and cook the mushrooms until they release their moisture.
  2. Add garlic, salt, and pepper, then finish with herbs.
  3. Serve over toast for a simple and satisfying meal.
